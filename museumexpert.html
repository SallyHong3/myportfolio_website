MuseumExpert: Branding & Web Design

Project Overview

Client: MuseumExpert.org

My Role: UX Designer, UI Designer

Timeline: June 2021 - Present

Tools: Sketch, Figma, Wix.com, HTML5, CSS, Canva, Miro, UsabilityHub, Google Form, Zoom

About:

MuseumExpert.org strives to help museum professionals who are out of work because of the COVID-19 pandemic. It provides a platform for employers with immediate, long- or short-term staffing needs to connect with experienced museum experts who are open to work.

The Challenge:

1. How to make MuseumExpert stand out among its competitors in the field with service that address users needs and a stronger online presence?
2. How to make the platform more intuitive for new users and make the expert searching process more efficient for employers?

The Solutions:

1. Establish a clear brand identity with a design style guide and branding strategies to increase brand recognition.
2. A redesigned and simplified landing page for the website with strong CTA buttons.
3. Redesign the information architecture of the website and include a top-level navigation bar.

The Prospective Result:

1. Develop a resuable design style guide for web-developers and content strategists, improving the current team work flows.
2. Improve user flows on sign-up, expert profile editing, and employers search & filter function.
3. An easily browsed landing page with high-level previews of MuseumExpert services.
4. Launch the desktop prototype with an increase of new users sign-ups and higher member retention rate.
